# Monetization System

This document captures PicknBuild's monetization model. Monetization should not block discovery. It should attach to advanced actions and intent.

## 19.1 Dealer Revenue

- **Per-lead unlock:** $15 per lead unlock.
- **Dealer subscription:** $99 per month.
- Subscription may include:
  - More visibility
  - More listings
  - Profile enhancements
  - Priority placement

| Item | Price |
| --- | --- |
| Lead unlock | $15 per lead |
| Dealer subscription | $99 / month |

## 19.2 Listing Revenue

- Individuals: 1 free listing.
- Dealers: 10 free listings.
- Additional listings: $5 each.

| User type | Free listings | Additional listing price |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Individual | 1 | $5 each |
| Dealer | 10 | $5 each |

## 19.3 picknbuild Revenue

- $1000 refundable deposit to start.
- Service margin from fulfilled builds.

## 19.4 Auction DIY Support Revenue

- Flat service fee, approximately $1000.
- May include:
  - Bidding support
  - Logistics coordination
  - Related services

## 19.5 Messaging Control as Monetization

- Individuals must message on-platform.
- Dealers can have partial open access, but full lead data requires payment.

## Rule

> Monetization should not block discovery. It should attach to advanced actions and intent.
