# PicknBuild Overview

## Definition

PicknBuild is a real-time, location-aware decision engine for car buying that:

- aggregates supply from all major sources
- calculates true acquisition outcomes
- enables customization
- captures missing supply
- tracks transactions
- routes users to the optimal path for getting a car

PicknBuild is **not** a simple marketplace and **not** just a listing site. It is the system that helps people understand **how to get a car**, not just **which car to click on**.

## Core Principle

PicknBuild does not show cars in isolation.

It shows:

- all ways to get a car
- true total cost, not just sticker price
- down payment requirements
- timeline to ownership
- effort required
- risk level
- customization possibilities
- what is realistically possible based on the user's financial situation, location, and urgency

## Core Insight

- The best option is **not the listing**.
- The best option is **the path**.

The market already has multiple ways to get the same car:

- dealer inventory
- auction inventory
- picknbuild as a done-for-you auction/customization path
- individual/private sellers

What is missing is the **decision layer** that compares those paths honestly and clearly.

## Final Statement

PicknBuild is a system that:

- aggregates supply
- structures decisions
- calculates outcomes
- enables customization
- captures missing supply
- connects people
- tracks intent
- learns from transactions
- routes users to the best path
