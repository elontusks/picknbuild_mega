# Dealer APR and Financing Logic

## APR Tiers

| Credit Score | APR |
|-------------|----:|
| 720–850 | 12% |
| 621–719 | 19.5% |
| 0–620 | 27% |

If below threshold: **show not approved state**.

## Changing Term Must Update

- estimated down payment
- loan amount
- estimated monthly
- estimated total paid
- interest paid

APR cost must be reflected in total paid.
