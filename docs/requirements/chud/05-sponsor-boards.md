# Path-Specific Sponsor Boards

Convert support/service sections into future sponsor boards.

These are **not generic ads**. They are structured sponsor zones **by path**.

## Dealer Support
- **Description:** Warranty, insurance, GAP coverage, and financing support
- **Secondary line:** Businesses can place services here

## DIY Support
- **Description:** Parts, tools, transport, and repair services
- **Secondary line:** Businesses can place services here

## picknbuild Protection
- **Description:** Insurance, warranty, and protection services
- **Secondary line:** Businesses can place services here

## Private Sale Protection
- **Description:** Inspection, payment protection, and title services
- **Secondary line:** Businesses can place services here

## Treatment Rule

Keep compact and integrated. **No banner-ad treatment.**
